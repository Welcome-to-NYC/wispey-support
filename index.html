FAQ

How does AI classification work?

wispey uses Apple Intelligence to automatically sort your memos into categories like To-do, Schedule, Ideas, and more. All processing happens on your device — nothing is sent to external servers.

Do I need Apple Intelligence?

No. wispey works without Apple Intelligence. When available, AI classification activates automatically. Without it, you can still write and organize memos manually.

Is iCloud sync required?

No. iCloud sync is optional and can be turned on or off anytime in Settings. Your memos are stored locally by default.

Which devices are supported?

wispey supports iPhone (iOS 17+) and Mac (macOS 14+).
